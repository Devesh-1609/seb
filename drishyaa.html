Drishyaa

Drishyaa is not just a platform—it is a movement toward self-growth, confidence, and excellence, empowering every individual to lead, inspire, and succeed.

About Drishyaa

The Social Engineers' Board was established by students of MMMEC, Naveen Krishna Rai (ECE, 2016 passout) and Mohit Jha (ECE, 2016 passout) in the year 2013. With the motto of Grooming Personality, the board aims at not only refining the engineering graduates academically, but holistically. It believes in the collective responsibility of the student fraternity towards the betterment of society and thus, constantly works in the direction of achieving this cause. The board tries to achieve its goals by a series of various events conducted throughout the academic year, both inside and outside the university.

Vision of Drishyaa

The vision of Drishyaa is to empower students with the knowledge, confidence, and direction required to make informed decisions about their future. It aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world opportunities by providing guidance, mentorship, and exposure to various career paths.

Interactive Learning Experience

Through interactive sessions, discussions, and collaborative activities, students gain practical insights, develop critical thinking skills, and learn from real-life experiences shared by industry experts and mentors.

Career Guidance & Opportunities

Students receive guidance on:

  • Career planning and goal setting
  • Higher education opportunities
  • Competitive examinations
  • Industry trends and emerging technologies
  • Essential professional skills

Personal Growth & Leadership

Drishyaa focuses not only on career development but also on personal growth. Participants are encouraged to build confidence, improve communication skills, develop leadership qualities, and cultivate a mindset of continuous learning and self-improvement.
