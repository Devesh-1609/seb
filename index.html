The Social Engineers' Board is a student-led society at MMMUT dedicated to fostering holistic personal and professional growth through community action and skill refinement.

Our Mission

To guide students towards their career goals through mentorship and support.

Our Community

A vibrant network where ideas are shared and friendships are built.

Our Vision

To empower every student to become a confident and successful professional.

Our Values

Collaboration, Integrity, and a commitment to helping one another succeed.

What We Offer

We provide a range of activities designed to build skills, broaden perspectives, and prepare you for the professional world.

Career Counselling

Get personalized guidance from seniors and industry experts to map out your career path.

Discussions & Debates

Engage in stimulating conversations on current affairs, technology, and more to sharpen your critical thinking.

Skill Workshops

Participate in workshops on public speaking, resume building, interview skills, and more.

Guest Lectures

Learn from the experiences of successful professionals and alumni.

Technical Projects

Collaborate on projects to gain hands-on experience and build your portfolio.

Community Service

Give back to the community through various social initiatives and outreach programs.
The Social Engineers' Board was established by students of MMMEC, Naveen Krishna Rai (ECE, 2016 passout) and Mohit Jha (ECE, 2016 passout) in the year 2013. With the motto of Grooming Personality, the board aims at not only refining the engineering graduates academically, but holistically. It believes in the collective responsibility of the student fraternity towards the betterment of society and thus, constantly works in the direction of achieving this cause. The board tries to achieve its goals by a series of various events conducted throughout the academic year, both inside and outside the university
